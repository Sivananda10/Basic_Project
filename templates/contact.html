Frequently Asked Questions

Is the service really free?
Yes, 100%. HobbyPredictor is an academic project and is completely free to use. No credit card required.
Is my child's data stored?
Input data is stored only to show your prediction history. It is never shared with third parties.
How accurate is the prediction?
Our Random Forest model achieves 92.5% accuracy on the test set. Confidence scores are shown with each result.
What age range is supported?
The model is trained on children aged 5 to 17 years and works best within that range.
